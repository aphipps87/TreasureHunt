## PROJECT REQUIREMENTS:

The land is divided into a grid of dangerous zones, each with a risk level (a positive number).
Your goal is to navigate from the top-left corner (0,0) to the bottom-right corner (n-1, m-1) while minimizing the total risk you accumulate along the way.

You can only move right or down at any step.
- Each cell contains a risk value, and stepping on it adds to your total risk.
- Your mission is to find the safest path to reach the treasure with the lowest possible total risk.

Complete three different approaches to solving the problem:
1. Recursive Backtracking (Brute Force)
2. Memoization (Top-Down Dynamic Programming)
3. Tabulation (Bottom-Up Dynamic Programming)
